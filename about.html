about me

Hi, I'm Dhruv, I am a multi-talented person with experience in a wide range of disciplines.

I am a final year computer science student and an aspiring front-end developer, I have over 2+ years of experience as a freelance front-end developer and is skilled in writing maintainable and scalable code, responsive design, and optimizing web performance. I spend most of my time experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (and its frameworks, mostly react).

I am also multi-disciplinary with experience of over 3+ years as a Manager, Co-founder, Advisor, Social worker, Teacher, Writer, and Researcher.

I do not like to confine my skill set to my degree hence I have a wide variety of skills apart from my majors, I have a keen interest in entrepreneurship, and to pursue my interest I have started various business model based projects in my college, most of these projects are social entrepreneurship projects, I consider my self as an environmentalist hence I try to inculcate the essence of sustainability in every project I start.

My other interest involves psychology, meditation, and therapy, for which I have been running a therapy group for the last 3 months, it is a community of small groups of people, but we have members from all across the globe.

I love reading books on business, tech and self-improvement. You can find my book suggestions and my reading list on my GoodReads account

I am looking for any opportunities that can help me improve my skillset and knowledge in various fields, I love communicating with people and sharing ideas with them, and discuss a wide range of topics to learn about other people's culture, work ethics, and more.
